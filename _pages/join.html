Join LANCA

We are always looking for motivated students and researchers interested in unsteady aerodynamics, vortex dynamics, and scientific machine learning.

PhD students

Prospective PhD students should apply through the SEMTE graduate program at ASU. Strong candidates have a background in aerospace engineering, mechanical engineering, applied mathematics, or physics, and experience with programming (Julia, Python, or MATLAB).

Before applying, please send a brief email with your CV, transcripts, and a short statement of your research interests to {{ site.pi.email }}.

Postdoctoral researchers

We welcome postdoctoral applications from candidates with strong publication records in unsteady aerodynamics, vortex methods, or related fields. Funded positions are advertised on this page when available; we are also happy to support applications for independent fellowships.

Undergraduate and Master's research

ASU undergraduates and Master's students interested in research projects with the lab are encouraged to reach out, particularly those who have taken MAE 494 / 564.

Visiting researchers

We host visiting PhD students and academics for short or long stays. Please get in touch to discuss possibilities.
